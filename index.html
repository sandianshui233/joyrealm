HOW TO PLAY

Getting Started

Simply click the "Play Now" button or use the game frame above to begin your adventure. No downloads or installations required!

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys for movement, mouse for camera control, and space bar to jump. Additional controls will be introduced as you progress through the tutorial.

Objectives

Complete missions, collect resources, and defeat enemies to advance through the game. Each level presents new challenges and rewards.
