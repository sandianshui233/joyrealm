Funny Walk Fail Runr

GAME INFO

Challenge yourself in this hilarious walking simulator! Take control of a wobbly villain who constantly stumbles into comedic mishaps. Every step brings unpredictable tumbles and amusing collisions in this action-packed arcade game. Navigate through increasingly unstable paths as you help your clumsy character complete various challenges. With simple controls but challenging gameplay, Funny Walk Fail Runr delivers non-stop laughter and entertainment for players of all ages!

Category: action
Controls: Desktop: Control movement with mouse clicks. Mobile: Simply tap the screen to play. Easy to learn, endlessly entertaining!
