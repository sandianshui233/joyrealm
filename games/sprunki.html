ABOUT THIS GAME

Incredibox is a music app that lets you create your own music with the help of a merry crew of beatboxers. Choose your musical style among 8 impressive atmospheres and start to lay down, record and share your mix.

How to Play

Simply drag and drop icons onto the characters to make them produce sounds. Create your own music and rhythm by combining different sounds. Find the right combinations to unlock animated choruses and special features.
